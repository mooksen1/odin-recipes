Butter Chicken Recipe

Description

Butter chicken, or otherwise known as murgh makhani is a dish that originates from India, and is one of their most well known dishes across the world. It is a curry dish made from spices, tomatoes, and butter, giving it a rich and creamy texture.

Ingredients

For the Chicken
  • 300 grams chicken breast
  • 1 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp Red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • Olive oil to pan fry
For the gravy
  • 500 grams roughly slit tomatoes
  • 100 grams red onions
  • 1 tbsp garlic paste
  • 50 grams cashews
  • 1 tsp kasoori methi
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala powder
  • 4 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp chilli powder (preferably kashmiri)
  • 5 tbsp butter
  • 3 tbsp cream
  • 2 tbsp malt vinegar
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Combine chicken, ginger garlic paste, red chili powder, and salt in a bowl. Let marinate for at least 20 minutes, the longer the better.
  2. Pan fry the chicken until cooked, being careful to not overcook, then set aside.
  3. In the same pan add butter and pan fry the onions until translucent, and then add garlic paste.
  4. Add the tomatoes cashew nuts, a small amount of water, salt, malt vinegar, sugar, garam masala powder, and chili powder. Evenly mix and let simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  5. Using a blender or food processor, blend the mix until it is the consistency of a puree.
  6. Use a fine strainer to strain the mixture back into the pan to remove the tomato seeds and skin. Make sure there is as little wasteage as possible!
  7. Add the butter, cream, chicken, and kasoori methi and let it simmer for another 10 minutes.
  8. I'd reccomend serving with basmati rice and naan. Now eat and enjoy!
