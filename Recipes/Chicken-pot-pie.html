One Skillet Chicken Pot Pie Recipe

Description

This is a delicious, comforting and creamy one pot chicken pot pie recipe and i'm gonna show you how to make it. side note: I would strongly recommend using a cast iron skillet or any kind of pan that can go into the oven.

I am also going to add that this recipe only puts a pie crust on top, instead on enclosed in a shell like most chicken pot pie recipes. I find it less doughy and it is my personal preference, but feel free to use a pie shell instead and make it more traditionally. The filling is the most important part!

Ingredients

  • 1 whole shredded rotisserie chicken
  • 1 1/2 cup frozen mixed veggies
  • 1 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 stick butter
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp italian seasoning
  • 1 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 egg
  • 1 pie crust

Steps

  1. Using butter, pan fry onions until translucent.
  2. Add flour, chicken broth, frozen veggies and chicken into the pot.
  3. Add heavy cream, and all our seasonings mentioned earlier, mix well and that's the filling done!
  4. If you didn't make the filling in a cast iron skillet already, move it now into a skillet, or any oven safe pan.
  5. Now unroll the pie crust and put it on top of your filling. Crimp the sides with a fork. Make sure to poke a few holes in the top!
  6. Scramble an egg in a bowl and brush on your egg wash onto your pie crust.
  7. Put your pie in the oven at 400, and bake for 40 minutes.
  8. That's it! The house should be smelling amazing by now! dig in and enjoy!
