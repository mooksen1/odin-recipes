Broccoli Cheddar Soup Recipe

Description

The perfect comfort food, broccoli cheddar soup. Rich, creamy, and loaded with cheese this recipe will leave you wanting more!

Ingredients

  • 1 head of broccoli, chopped
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 cups shredded carrots
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese (can add more if you want)
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 stick butter
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp red chili flakes

Steps

  1. In a pot add your butter and fry your onions, garlic and add flour.
  2. Add your chicken broth, broccoli, and carrots to the pot.
  3. Add your garlic powder, paprika, salt, pepper, and red chili flakes and mix well.
  4. Pour in your heavy cream, mix well and let simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  5. When your soup has thickened add your cheese, and mix it up.
  6. Your soup should be all ready! make some garlic bread, and enjoy this hearty recipe!
